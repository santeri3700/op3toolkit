Usage: op3toolkit [OPTION] [FILE]

  --oem-unlock		Unlock bootloader
  --flash-twrp		Flash default TWRP
  --flash-recovery	Flash recovery from [FILE]
  --supersu		Push newest SuperSU zip to /sdcard
  --help		Display this help and exit
  --version		Display version and exit
